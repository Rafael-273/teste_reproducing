Local Gatherings

RCN Gatherings – Ministry Leader Gatherings

RCN Gatherings are local in-person gatherings that are the “bread and butter” of the network. Ministry leaders come together to pray for one another and for the evangelization of their area, encourage each other through authenticity, learn from one another, and plan together to missionally engage their region for Christ. All gatherings are organized, led, and facilitated by local ministry leaders.

Join a gathering: Thanks for inquiring! Fill out the contact form and one of our leaders will reach out to you.

Start a gathering: You'll need at least two other ministry leaders committed, have viewed the leader facilitator training, and connected with an RCN Gathering Coach for next steps.

Live Sent Gatherings – Marketplace Leader Gatherings

Live Sent Gatherings are local in-person gatherings for marketplace leaders. Like the RCN Gatherings, leaders come together to pray, encourage, learn, and collaborate for missional engagement in their community. All gatherings are led and facilitated by local marketplace leaders.

Join a gathering: Thanks for inquiring! Fill out the contact form and one of our leaders will reach out to you.

Start a gathering: You'll need at least two other marketplace leaders committed, have viewed the leader facilitator training, and connected with a Live Sent Gathering Coach for next steps.
