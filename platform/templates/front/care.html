Care

We care about ministry leaders. Pastors and ministry leaders are feeling the daily stress and burden of ministry. In fact, many are depressed, lonely and hurting. In recent days, pastors are resigning from churches in unprecedented ways. Know that we are here to help! We recommend two ministries at Reproducing Churches: Shepherd Rest and Shepherd’s Help. We partner with them to give the needed help pastors seek.

Pastoral Support

Free Counseling with Shepherd’s Help

If you are struggling and need ongoing support for the challenges you are facing in your life and ministry, we recommend that you take advantage of the free counseling services of Shepherd’s Help. There you will meet accredited counselors who will help you without cost.

Go to shepherdshelp.org to learn more

Restorative Retreats with Shepherd Rest

If you and your spouse are going through a time of extraordinary crisis, we recommend that you apply for one of Shepherd Rest retreats. As a couple, you’ll have the opportunity to rest, renew and refocus. These personal, customized retreats are intended to give ministry couples encouragement and a plan for facing their challenges.

Visit shepherdrest.com to apply
