Church Planter

Pathway

Perhaps you have sensed God moving in you to start a new church. We have a development process that will prepare you. Starting a new church can be daunting and difficult, but we are here to walk with you every step of the way. For RCN church planters, pathway costs are underwritten by the network.

Step 01: RCN Orientation

expand_circle_down

Learn in depth the vision, values, mission, beliefs, ethos and multiplying leader approach of Reproducing Churches. Engage with others who will help you on the journey and support you in the process. Scheduled appointment. Time: 2 hours.

Step 02: Discover Church Planting

expand_circle_down

Understand the key characteristics and behaviors of successful church planters. Learn the overview of church planting and hear stories of those who have planted churches. Meet others who are exploring a call to start a new church. This is a scheduled event. Time: 3 hours.

Step 03: Engage Church Planting

expand_circle_down

Begin the process of forming a plan for a prayer team, raising funds, sharing your faith, and engaging the community strategically. This is a one-day training. Time: 8 hours.

Step 04: Church Planter Assessment

expand_circle_down

This is a time of personal evaluation of your ability to start a new church by experienced church planters. This evaluation is partly based on the homework accomplished in the Engage Church Planting class and your personality inventories. This is a one-day event. Time: 8 hours. Some pre-work involved.

Step 05: Church Plant Strategy

expand_circle_down

Develop an overally strategy for your church plant and the related corresponding systems and processes to undergird your strategy approach. Work completed from Engage Church Planting and your assessment will be valuable for this learning. Once completed you will be assigned a church planting coach. This is a two-day event. Time is 16 hours.

Ongoing Support

RCN Gatherings

Attend an ongoing Reproducing Churches Network Gathering in your area to pray, encourage and collaborate with others to establish new expressions of the church to reach people for Christ in your area. Network Groups meet monthly or bi-monthly. Time is 2 hours.

Coaching

Meet with your assigned coach regularly to talk though challenges and opportunities in your new church. Construct possible solutions and initiatives to move the new church forward. Coaching is monthly. Time is one hour.

Funding

Every new church plant will be evaluated for possible funding grants. Evaluation for funding is based on the progress of the church plant. Application for financial assistance is given by the church planter coach. These are one-time grants given successively as milepost are completed.

Ongoing Training

Regular webinars, in person trainings, multiplying leader cohorts and the Annual Live Sent Conference are available. Check the calendar for upcoming events.
