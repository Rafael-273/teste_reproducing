Existing Church

Pathway

This pathway is designed for established churches looking to multiply, revitalize, or expand their impact. With guided steps and continued support, your church can experience a new wave of mission, outreach, and transformation. Let’s partner together for Kingdom growth.

Step 01: RCN Orientation

expand_circle_down

Learn in depth the vision, values, mission, beliefs, ethos and multiplying leader approach of Reproducing Churches. Engage with others who will help you on the journey and support you in the process. Scheduled appointment. Time: 2 hours. No cost.

Step 02: Review Of Church Health

expand_circle_down

Enlist a Reproducing Churches Consultant to help you assess and evaluate your church’s health. Organizational and Leadership development reccomendations are made. Next Step is attending the Church Blueprint Training. Optional services available. Cost varies. Time: one day consult is standard.

Step 03: Church Blueprint: Strategy and Systems for Multiplication

expand_circle_down

Develop an overally strategy for your church. Review systems and processes to undergird your new strategy approach. Work completed from your church evaluation will be applied in this training. This is a two-day event. Time is 16 hours

Step 04: Coaching

expand_circle_down

Meet with your RCN coach to talk though challenges and opportunities in your execution of the church strategy blueprint. Receive encouragement and ideas to keep you and your church moving forward.

Step 05: Ongoing Training and Gatherings

expand_circle_down

Attend RCN Gatherings in your area. Take advantage of regular webinars, in person trainings, multiplying leader cohorts and the Annual Live Sent Conference are available. Check the calendar for upcoming events and gatherings.
