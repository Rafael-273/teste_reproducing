Consulting

Unlock the full potential of your church's ministry with the invaluable guidance of a church vitality consultant. You'll gain deep insights and practical strategies tailored to breathe new life into your congregation and expand your mission.

WHY CONSULTING?

Consulting is more than revitalizing—it's about multiplying. Through strategic planning and discipleship-driven growth, consultants help churches break traditional limits and foster thriving communities of disciple-makers.

Partnering with Reproducing Churches means embracing exponential growth through disciple-making, leadership development, and church multiplication. Our consultants equip you for long-term impact through a biblically rooted and strategic approach.

With our unique P5 Consulting Model, you’ll clarify key aspects of your ministry:

  1. Perspective: Understand your ministry's scope and challenges.
  2. Purpose: Refine your mission and what you aim to accomplish.
  3. Priorities: Identify critical areas that need attention.
  4. Plan: Develop a strategic, intentional roadmap.
  5. Process: Create a system to implement your strategy daily.

When P5 is aligned, the result is Clarity, Focus, Growth, Impact, and Change.

P5 CONSULTING PACKAGE

Churches who commit to the P5 process will receive three 2-day onsite consultations every 6 months, plus monthly coaching. Each consultation includes framework development, strategic planning, and evaluation.

Need more access? Custom plans are available for ongoing support tailored to your needs.
